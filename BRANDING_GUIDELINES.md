# AIChatBotFree Brand Guidelines

## 1) Brand Personality
- **Voice & Tone:** Confident, helpful, and practical. Avoid jargon when unnecessary; explain AI and chatbot topics in plain, actionable language.
- **Positioning:** Trusted guide for evaluating free vs paid AI chatbot solutions across industries.
- **Values:** Transparency (disclose affiliates), rigor (objective testing), clarity (structured comparisons), and accessibility (content usable by non-technical readers).
- **Visual Feel:** Modern SaaS, crisp layouts, generous whitespace, and purposeful color pops to highlight actions and comparisons.

## 2) Logo Usage Rules
- **Clear Space:** Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the capital "A" in the logotype on all sides.
- **Minimum Size:** Digital minimum width of 120px; print minimum width of 30mm to preserve legibility.
- **Backgrounds:** Prefer Ultra Light Gray (#F5F7FA) or white for maximum contrast. When on photos, place the logo over a 70–80% opaque navy (#0A1A2F) overlay.
- **Do:**
  - Use provided vector assets (SVG/AI) without altering proportions.
  - Keep sufficient contrast between logo and background (WCAG AA+).
  - Use monochrome white logo on dark backgrounds and electric blue on light backgrounds.
- **Don’t:**
  - Stretch, skew, or recolor outside the approved palette.
  - Place on visually busy backgrounds without an overlay.
  - Add shadows, outlines, or effects that change the logo appearance.

## 3) Color Palette & Usage

### Primary Colors
- **Electric Blue** `#0066FF` — Primary action color, links, key CTAs, and highlights.
- **Deep Navy** `#0A1A2F` — Base text on light backgrounds, headers, navigation, cards.
- **Ultra Light Gray** `#F5F7FA` — Page backgrounds, card backgrounds, table rows, and subtle dividers.

### Secondary Colors
- **Emerald** `#00C27A` — Success states, positive badges, accent buttons.
- **Amber** `#F9A825` — Warnings, attention banners, limited-time offers.
- **Crimson** `#E53935` — Errors, destructive actions, critical alerts.

### Usage Mapping
- **Backgrounds:** Primary background is Ultra Light Gray; Navy for hero/footers; Electric Blue gradients optional for feature highlights.
- **Buttons:**
  - Primary: Electric Blue text on white or white text on Electric Blue.
  - Secondary: Navy outline on white or Navy fill with white text.
  - Tertiary: Text-only links in Navy; hover to Electric Blue.
- **Alerts:** Success (Emerald/white), Warning (Amber/Navy text), Error (Crimson/white text or Crimson outline on Light Gray).
- **Borders & Dividers:** Ultra Light Gray for subtle dividers; Navy 10% opacity for card borders; Electric Blue for focus rings.
- **Data Viz / Badges:** Use Secondary colors to distinguish statuses; avoid overusing Crimson.

## 4) Typography System
- **Primary Typeface:** Inter (or system fallback: "Inter", "Segoe UI", "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif).
- **Heading Scale (desktop / mobile):**
  - H1: 40/32px, weight 700, line-height 1.2
  - H2: 32/28px, weight 700, line-height 1.25
  - H3: 26/22px, weight 600, line-height 1.3
  - H4: 22/20px, weight 600, line-height 1.35
  - H5: 18/17px, weight 600, line-height 1.4
  - H6: 16/15px, weight 600, line-height 1.45
- **Body Text:**
  - Body Large: 18px, weight 400, line-height 1.6
  - Body Base: 16px, weight 400, line-height 1.6
  - Captions/Meta: 14px, weight 500, line-height 1.5
- **Spacing:** Use 24–32px above headings and 12–16px below; paragraphs spaced at 12px with max line length ~75 characters.
- **Links:** Navy default, Electric Blue hover/active; underline on hover for accessibility.

## 5) Iconography Guidelines
- **Style:** Simple, outline or duotone icons with consistent stroke weight (1.5–2px). Rounded corners to align with button radii.
- **Colors:** Navy for default line icons; Electric Blue for active/hover; Emerald/Amber/Crimson for status icons matching alert types.
- **Sizes:** 20–24px for UI controls; 32–48px for feature highlights; maintain consistent padding.
- **Do/Don’t:** Keep icons centered in a 44px min touch target; avoid drop shadows or complex fills.

## 6) UI Components
- **Buttons:**
  - Primary: Electric Blue background, white text, 8px radius, 14–16px padding. Hover: darken to #0057d6; Focus: 2px Electric Blue outline on a 2px translucent Navy ring.
  - Secondary: Navy background with white text or Navy outline with Navy text. Hover: lighten fill or increase border contrast.
  - Tertiary/Text: Navy text; hover to Electric Blue.
- **Cards:** Light Gray background, 12–16px radius, subtle shadow (`0 10px 30px rgba(10,26,47,0.08)`), 20–24px padding. Use Navy text; highlight links in Electric Blue.
- **Form Fields:** Light Gray background, 8px radius, 14px padding. Border in Navy 10% opacity; focus border Electric Blue with 2px glow. Labels in Navy 80% opacity.
- **Alerts:**
  - Success: Emerald background, white text, optional darker Emerald icon.
  - Warning: Amber background with Navy text/icon; ensure 4.5:1 contrast.
  - Error: Crimson background with white text; outline variant on Light Gray for subtle contexts.
- **Comparison Tables:** Use alternating Light Gray rows; headers in Navy bold 600; highlight key cells with Electric Blue text.

## 7) Layout & Spacing Rules
- **Grid & Spacing:** Adopt a 4pt/8pt system. Base unit 4px; standard spacing increments: 8, 12, 16, 20, 24, 32, 40, 48, 64px.
- **Container Widths:** Desktop max width 1200–1280px; gutter 24px; mobile padding 16px.
- **Corner Radius:** Buttons 8px; cards and inputs 12–16px; modals 16–20px.
- **Elevation:** Use subtle shadows for interactive elements; avoid stacking more than two elevation levels on a page.

## 8) Accessibility Guidelines
- **Contrast:** Ensure text meets WCAG AA (4.5:1 for body, 3:1 for large text). Electric Blue on Light Gray meets AA; Navy on Light Gray exceeds AA; use white text on Crimson/Emerald only if contrast ≥4.5:1 (adjust shade if necessary).
- **Focus States:** Always show visible focus rings (Electric Blue + translucent Navy outline). Do not remove outlines.
- **Touch Targets:** Minimum 44px by 44px for interactive elements on touch devices.
- **Link States:** Distinguishable hover/active states; avoid relying on color alone—add underline or weight change.
- **Motion:** Keep accordion/hover animations under 200ms with `ease-out`; provide reduced-motion respect via `prefers-reduced-motion` where possible.

## 9) Component Color Mapping (Quick Reference)
- **Hero/Navigation:** Background Deep Navy, text Light Gray/White, primary CTA Electric Blue.
- **Cards/Sections:** Background Ultra Light Gray, headings Navy, links Electric Blue.
- **Buttons:** Primary Electric Blue; Secondary Navy or outline; Destructive Crimson; Success Emerald.
- **Alerts:** Success Emerald + White text; Warning Amber + Navy text; Error Crimson + White text; Info/Neutral Light Gray + Navy text.
- **Borders/Dividers:** Light Gray (#F5F7FA) for subtle separation; Navy 10–15% opacity for stronger delineation.

## 10) Where the Brand Colors Appear in the Theme
- **Global shell:** Body background uses Deep Navy with subtle Electric Blue/Emerald radial accents; text defaults to Light Gray for contrast.
- **Header/nav:** Sticky header with translucent Deep Navy; nav hover states lift to Electric Blue. Logo/wordmark should use Electric Blue on light or white on dark.
- **Hero:** Gradient overlay combines Electric Blue and Emerald tints on a Deep Navy base; primary CTA buttons are Electric Blue → Navy gradients with white text, secondary buttons are soft Navy outlines.
- **Category folders:** Top “tab” bar uses Electric Blue by default (or the per-card accent color from ACF), giving an obvious brand hit above the folder grid.
- **Pillars/cards/tables:** Cards rest on Deep Navy/Ultra Light Gray mixes with Electric Blue links and Emerald success badges; comparison table hover states use subtle Electric Blue tint.
- **Ratings/alerts:** Star ratings and highlight badges lean on Amber/Emerald; error/destructive patterns use Crimson for clarity.
- **Article sections module:** Accordion headers, tick/cross icons, CTA buttons, and section backgrounds pull from the CSS variables set to the brand palette, so flexible layouts inherit the same Electric Blue/Emerald accents.
